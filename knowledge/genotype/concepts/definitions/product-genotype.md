# Product Genotype

## 1. Genotype — General Understanding

**Genotype** is a vector of pairs Problem-Solution that contains very valuable information about client needs and our ways of addressing them. It is a core concept for understanding the fit between what customers want and what we offer.

The Genotype serves as a structured representation of needs and solutions, allowing us to compare demand and supply in a systematic way.

The Genotype has two vectors: the Customer Genotype (demand vector) and the Product Genotype (supply vector) much like a DNA helix.

---

## 2. Customer Genotype

**Customer Genotype** is the demand vector — a long list of needs that the client has expressed.

We collect the client genotype through user observations, inteviews, questions, market research.

Our goal is to understand:

1. **What** the client wants
2. **How strong** each need is — the level and intensity of the need

The levels of need are defined by the `genotype-needs-taxonomy.md` 

---

## 3. Product Genotype

**Product Genotype** is the supply vector. It is oriented in the same way as the client genotype, but specifies **how well we satisfy each need** in our product.

For each client need (in the customer genotype), we describe how our product addresses it:

We compare our solutions within the product to each client need, mapping our capabilities to the demand vector.

The levels of feature fidelity are defined by the `genotype-fidelity-taxonomy.md` 

---

## 4. Problem-Solution Fit

**Problem-Solution Fit** is the coincidence (alignment) between:

- The vector of needs — **client genotype**
- Our vector of solutions — **product genotype**

If we solve the sharp needs of the client well, we achieve a very good problem-solution fit.

**The entrepreneur's task:**

1. Understand the client genotype well — the vector of needs
2. Design the product so it has a strong product genotype — good solutions for those needs
3. Achieve a very good problem-solution fit

When we do this, we can sell a rationally high-quality product to our customers.
